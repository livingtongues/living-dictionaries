# Privacy Policy (BEFORE — none existed for the app)

There was **no Living Dictionaries privacy policy** before this proposal. The app's Terms of Use
linked the Living Tongues organizational policy at https://livingtongues.org/privacy-policy/, which
covers the WordPress marketing site (blog comments, Jetpack, MailChimp newsletter, donations) — **not
the Living Dictionaries application** (its auth, local database, media hosting, telemetry, or inbound
email).

Because there is no comparable prior document, the entire proposed Privacy Policy reads as **new** in
the comparison on the right — please review it in full rather than as a set of edits.
