### The one real bug — waveform decode on recorded audio (🟡 P3)

`Waveform.svelte:80` `console.error('Unable to decode audio for waveform', error)` fired **13×** for 2
real contributors — **Android 10 / Chrome** and **Mac OS X / Safari** — both mid-**recording**
(breadcrumbs: *Prepare to Record → Tap to Record → Stop Recording*). `decode_audio_buffer()` (Web
Audio `decodeAudioData`) rejects on the just-recorded blob, so the peaks never render. **Playback is
unaffected** — it uses a plain `<audio>` element (the 07-12 static-canvas rewrite). So this is a
cosmetic "no waveform on my new recording" for those two, not a data-loss or blocking bug. **But the
`error` serializes to `{}`** — we can't see whether it's an unsupported MIME (Safari's mp4/aac vs
Chrome's webm/opus), an empty blob, or a genuine codec gap. **Fix = enrich the log first, then
decide** (§5). Not urgent; do it next time the audio path is touched.
