### The one new real bug â€” `props_invalid_value` on the structured-grammar editor (ðŸŸ¡ P3, preview-gated)

Fired **6Ã—** at 13:20 UTC in **Jacob's** (`jwrunner7@gmail.com`, L3) session on `/rhenic/grammar`
(build `1784115714875`), breadcrumb trail `/rhenic â†’ /rhenic/agents â†’ grammar`. `/rhenic/grammar`
runs the **brand-new structured-grammar UI** (cutover `356dd77d`, 03:24 UTC today; pass-1/2
`07f0041c`/`658a46ba`) â€” admin-3-gated, so **only L3 admins can reach it; zero real-user exposure.**

**Root cause (pinned, not guessed):** `SectionEditor.svelte` renders per-locale editors with
`bind:value={draft_body[bcp]}` and `bind:value={draft_usage[bcp]}`, where
`draft_body = $state({ ...(section.body || {}) })` is a MultiString map. For a locale `bcp` the section
has **no** body/usage in, `draft_body[bcp]` is `undefined`. The child `MarkdownEditor.svelte` declares
`value = $bindable('')` â€” a **non-undefined fallback**. Svelte 5 throws
[`props_invalid_value`](https://svelte.dev/e/props_invalid_value) precisely when you `bind:` an
`undefined` into a `$bindable` prop that has a fallback default. (The sibling `bind:value={draft_title[bcp]}`
is safe â€” it targets a native `<input>`, no fallback; `ClauseSlotPicker` is safe too â€” its `value` uses
`$bindable()` with no fallback.)

**Fix direction (for when Jacob next touches grammar â€” not applied here):** either drop the `''`
fallback on `MarkdownEditor`'s bindable (`value = $bindable()`), or pre-seed the current `bcp` key in
`draft_body`/`draft_usage` to `''` before rendering that locale's editor. Low-risk, one-line either way.
