## Deliberately not proposed

- `page.direction = ltr` in German is functional metadata, not untranslated prose.
- Product names, `ID`, `URL`, `PDF`, `CSV`, `AI`, and familiar media loanwords were retained where they are intentional.
- Swahili `misc.theme_light = Nyepesi` initially looked like the “lightweight” sense, but current Swahili software localization also uses `hali nyepesi` opposite `hali ya giza`; it is not strong enough to call an error.
- Arabic `grammar.add_grammar = إضافة نحو` is terse and could be expanded, but `نحو` is a legitimate linguistic term. Without native reviewer preference, changing it would cross into terminology refinement rather than fixing a clear mistake.
- Several clause-slot translations use the user-facing equivalent of “sentence position” rather than a highly technical term for “clause slot.” They remain understandable and internally consistent, so no broad terminology rewrite is proposed.
